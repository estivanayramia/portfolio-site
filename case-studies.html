Savonie Laundry Management System

Transforming chaotic laundry operations into a streamlined digital workflow

The Challenge

Savonie's laundry operations relied on manual paper logs, sticky notes, and verbal handoffs. This led to lost items, unclear accountability, and frequent customer complaints. Staff spent hours reconciling physical logs with actual inventory.

The Solution

  • Built a digital tracking system with barcode scanning for instant check-in/out
  • Implemented automated status updates and customer notifications via SMS/email
  • Created real-time dashboard showing order status, bottlenecks, and team performance
  • Designed mobile-first interface for on-the-go access by delivery drivers
